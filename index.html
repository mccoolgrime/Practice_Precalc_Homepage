Speaking in Precalculus with Ms. M-G

Mathematics is a language that both shapes and describes powerful stories about the world around us through engineering, biomedical science, computer programming, data analysis and countless other fields. Precalculus is an immersion program. Our goal here is to give you the language skills to be successful anywhere you go in the Land of Mathematics.

By the end of the class, you will be able to:

Predict the slant asymptote of a rational runction using polynomial long division.
Calculate the finite sum of an infinite sequence by hand and using technology.
Animate parametric equations to create artistic designs in the desmos graphing calcultor.
Determine the height of a hot air balloon given a distance between two objects and the angles of depression measured when sighting those objects.
